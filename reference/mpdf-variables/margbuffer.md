<p>(mPDF &gt;= 5.5)</p>
<p>Allows an (empty) end of block to extend beyond the bottom margin by this amount (mm). Avoids just the border/background-color of the end of a block being moved on to next page.</p>
<p>DEFAULT 2</p>
